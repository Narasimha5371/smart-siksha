# Smart Shiksha - Feature Roadmap

## 1. Voice-First Interface (Accessibility)
- **Goal**: Enable students with lower literacy to interact verbally.
- **Tech**: Speech-to-Text (STT) and Text-to-Speech (TTS) models optimized for Indic languages.
- **Features**:
    - "Ask a Doubt": Voice queries like "Explain Newton's third law in Hindi".
    - Voice Navigation: Control app menus via voice commands.

## 2. Gamified Learning Path
- **Goal**: Increase engagement and retention.
- **Features**:
    - **XP System**: Earn points for lessons and quizzes.
    - **Streaks**: Daily learning incentives.
    - **Leaderboards**: Local (school-level) rankings to foster healthy competition.
    - **Badges**: Unlockable achievements (e.g., "Math Wizard", "Early Bird").

## 3. Peer-to-Peer "Study Circle"
- **Goal**: Collaborative learning.
- **Features**:
    - **Asynchronous Forum**: Post doubts (text/voice).
    - **Peer Verification**: Answers verified by AI or Teachers.
    - **Community XP**: Rewards for helping peers.

## 4. Snap-to-Solve (OCR & AI)
- **Goal**: digitize textbook questions.
- **Features**:
    - Camera integration to capture questions.
    - OCR to extract text.
    - AI step-by-step solution generation (not just the final answer).

## 5. Interactive Simulations
- **Goal**: Practical understanding of concepts.
- **Features**:
    - Lightweight 2D Physics/Chemistry engines.
    - Offline-capable interactive experiments (e.g., mixing chemicals, projectile motion).

## 6. Parent "Lite" View
- **Goal**: Parental involvement without complexity.
- **Features**:
    - **Daily SMS/WhatsApp Reports**: "Your child studied Physics for 45 mins today."
    - **Simple Dashboard**: Green/Red indicators for attendance and progress.
